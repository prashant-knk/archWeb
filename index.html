Introduction

K&K – Kala & Kriti is a premier architectural model-making company, renowned for turning blueprints into breathtaking physical realities. With an artistic soul and engineering finesse, we specialize in the design and construction of detailed architectural models that reflect vision, structure, and perfection.

Established over 30 years ago, we've become one of the most trusted and experienced players in the field, offering a perfect blend of artistic craftsmanship, modern technology, and precision engineering.

Our Domain Expertise

Residential Societies

At K&K – Residential society models are at the heart of our expertise. With over 500+ residential models successfully delivered, we have helped architects, builders, and developers visualize and present their housing concepts with precision and elegance.

  • Delivered 500+ residential society models across India and internationally
  • Expertise from small villa layouts to large integrated townships
  • Projects delivered in Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Dubai, and more

Schools & Educational Campuses

  • Delivered numerous educational campus models for schools, colleges, and universities
  • Expertise in K-12 schools, international institutions, engineering & medical colleges, and university master plans
  • Models showcase academic blocks, hostels, admin buildings, playgrounds, and auditoriums
  • Emphasis on functional zoning, student movement flow, and campus aesthetics

IT & Tech Corporate Parks

Materials That Define Quality

We believe that the quality of materials reflects the quality of vision. That's why K&K uses only the most reliable, durable, and visually appealing materials.

  • Acrylic Sheets – Smooth finish, clarity, and elegance
  • Foam Boards, MDF, and Balsa Wood – Ideal for structural base and 3D elements
  • High-Grade Plastic and PVC – For intricate detailing and long-term endurance
  • Transparent Panels – For illuminated internal layouts and lighting simulation
  • Eco-Friendly Options – Sustainable materials when requested

Our Technology Edge

We integrate advanced model-making technologies to bring designs to life with unmatched detail and realism.

  • Laser Cutting & Laser Engraving Machines – Perfect edge finishes and intricate patterns
  • 3D Printing Capabilities – Custom textures, small figures, and rapid prototyping
  • CNC Milling & CAD Software Integration – Digital-to-physical model transformation
  • LED Lighting & Transparent Views – Night-view simulation and internal illumination
